Millimeter-Scale Computing. Real-life smart dust.

The Cubisens platform by CubeWorks enables the first truly autonomous wireless sensing platform measuring less than a millimeter. Cubisens systems sense and process their environment, wirelessly transmitting results or storing them for later collection. The versaltility of the Cubisens platform allows multiple units to be combined together to tackle bigger monitoring and sensing applications.

{# Avoid padding edge of image #}

Redefining Ultra-Low Power. Enabling perpetual computing.

CubeWorks' systems average a record-setting 8 nW power draw in standby mode. In indoor ambient light, the 1 mm2 solar cell produces 10 nW, facilitating perpetual operation.

From the Internet of Things... to the Internet of Everything.

The small form factor, wireless capability, and long lifetime of Cubisens makes it an ideal platform for realizing ubiquitous computing. The first-generation systems are equipped with imaging, motion detection, temperature sensing, and pressure sensing. CubeWorks aims to expand the breadth of applications and position its Cubisens technology as pivotal for realizing the impending Internet of Everything.
